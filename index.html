About Ritesh Kapoor – Chartered Accountant

Ritesh Kapoor is an accomplished Chartered Accountant with extensive experience in delivering strategic financial solutions to businesses and entrepreneurs across diverse sectors. His professional journey is built on a foundation of integrity, commitment, and in-depth expertise in Indian and international accounting standards.

Over the years, Ritesh has advised start-ups, SMEs, multinational corporations, and non-profit organizations on critical areas including:

  • Company Incorporation & Business Structuring
  • Regulatory Compliances under FEMA, RBI, SEBI, and Companies Act
  • Virtual CFO and Advisory Services
